Project Description:

In recent years, Chicago has been receiving increasing numbers of rodent infestation complaints. It is important for the city government to actively engage in resolving rat complaints, as rats can cause huge property damage and spread harmful pathogens to humans. This interactive visualization project consists of a story section that illustrates the severity and patterns of rat complaints within the city of Chicago, and also a dashboard section for viewers to explore the rat complaints at a community level.
